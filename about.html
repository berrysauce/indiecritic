About

Hi, my name is Paul! I'm a german student and I love to play games - especially indie games. Now, what does "indie" even mean?

one that is independent
Merriam Webster

I find it amazing how these handcrafted games look and feel compared to the "factory produced" industry games. They're often so much more creative and each one tells its own stories.

Since I wanted to make it easy for people to discover indie games, I made this newsletter. I'm a little privacy fanatic myself so your email address is in good hands, and if you don't trust me, use a service like altmails, Feedbin, or  Stoop.
