For Publishers

It's possible to sponsor a review in an indiecritic issue. You'll benefit from attention by the readers since there are only a maximum of three reviews per issue (twice a month).

Please note that:

1. I will review the game as if I paid full price - you can't buy my oppinion

2. You'll be able to sponsor a review by providing me with a key to your game - no additional payments

3. It's not guaranteed that I review your game even if you give me a key - please check in with me before sending me a key

If you're ok with the above mentioned points, please send me an email to:

publishers@indiecritic.net
